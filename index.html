The Origin Story

From the mist of Niagara Falls to the frozen tundra of Orchard Park. A journey no scout predicted.

Niagara Falls, Ontario

Born to Compete

Growing up in the shadow of one of the world's natural wonders, young Ilan developed an unnatural arm strength from years of skipping stones across the Niagara River. Local legend says he once threw a football across the Horseshoe Falls. Nobody can confirm this. Nobody can deny it either.

McGill University, Montreal

Bachelor of Commerce (and Touchdowns)

While his classmates studied supply chain management, Ilan was building his own chain: Real Ventures, The Founder Project, and a legendary intramural flag football record that McGill administrators refuse to discuss publicly.

2013, Montreal Cafes

StackedSP is Born

Armed with a laptop and an espresso, Ilan founded StackedSP from a cafe in Montreal. The startup recruiting firm would grow to serve billion dollar companies. But the real recruiting was just beginning.

Toronto, The Brother Wars

Recruiting Jared Saks: A 2 Year Campaign

It took Ilan two full years of relentless persuasion to recruit his own brother Jared to StackedSP. NFL scouts later noted this was more impressive than any 40 yard dash time. "If he can close his own brother after 730 days of objections, imagine what he can do in the pocket," said one anonymous GM.

2025, Orchard Park

The Call from Buffalo

When the Bills' front office received a cold email from Ilan with the subject line "Re: Your Open QB Position," they assumed it was spam. Then they watched the tape. Then they cleared $847M in cap space. The rest is history being written in real time.

Ian, people said the same thing about my Wonderlic score. They said the same thing about my cold email open rates at StackedSP. I was built in the cold. The passion of Bills Mafia? That is StackedSP energy. Those people jump through tables for fun. I once jumped through a metaphorical table every day for two years trying to hire my brother. These are my people.
